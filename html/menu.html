Private Event Menus

Prices start at $100 per person for a 3 course meal. Typically includes an appetizer, an entree, and a dessert. Courses may be chosen from any of the menus. Any change to number of courses will affect per person price. It is customary to choose 1 dish per course for the whole group to eat. If there are allergy, dietary, or other restrictions, accommodations can be made.
