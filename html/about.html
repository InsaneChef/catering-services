About Me

My Mission for my local community:

To become a first class establishment serving small and medium sized events with haute cuisine in the greater Cleveland/Akron/Canton areas. I wish to cultivate my relationships with local farmers to provide my clients with high quality ingredients all grown/raised here in Ohio!

My Vision for the internet community:

Create multiple web applications from the ground up for recipes, menus, dishes with chef's notes, food hacks, a recipe web scraper, and possibly a chrome/firefox browser extension that all ties in with my physical business of providing personal chef and catering services.
