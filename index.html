Private Chef

This service is where I create specialized meals in your home or place of business according to your specifications. You can either choose from the menus I have posted here or let me create a custom menu for you at no extra charge. I will do all the shopping/purchasing, locally where possible and come to you for meal preperation. This service is an intimate choice that is great for anniversaries, birthdays, graduations, and engagements for 25 or less guests. Each dish is plated and served. Typically these are 3 courses and as each dish is presented I will explain what it is and can answer any questions. I will be on site for this from start to finish. If there are any dietary restrictions, allergies, or dish/ingredient preferences, it is not an issue for me to make those adjustments on the menu. Although prices vary, a general idea is $100 per person for a 3-4 course meal with a 25% deposit within 14 days of event.

Catering

This service is for small and medium sized catering events for up to 50 guests. Currently all menus for this are custom made, everything made in-house. Whether you have an outdoor BBQ and you want me there to operate the grill or just create some basic hors d'oeuvres for drop off, I am extremely flexible. Prices for this will vary greatly, anywhere from $15 per person up to $100+ per person, depending on menus and level of service/staff required. Local sourcing of ingredients is done where possible.
